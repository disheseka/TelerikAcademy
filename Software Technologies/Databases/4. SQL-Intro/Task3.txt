Start SQL Management Studio and connect to the database TelerikAcademy. 
Examine the major tables in the "TelerikAcademy" database.
	The major tables in DB "TelerikAcademy" are Employees, Departments, Addresses.
		Employees contains full information about every employee like first name, last name, salary, hire date and etc.
		Departments contains names of different departments in the company.
		Towns contains names of towns where departments are and where different employee works.
